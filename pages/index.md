## ðŸŒŸ Mission Statement

**Sentinema** goes beyond simple star ratings. It combines **Emotion Analysis** with **Visual Interpretation**.

By analyzing the emotional tone of reviews, Sentinema reveals *why* viewers liked or disliked a movie, creating a deeper connection between the audience and the content.

We aim to:
- Provide accessible, easy-to-use **Emotion Classification Models**.
- Visualize text data into intuitive **Word Clouds**.
- Encourage open-source contribution through our **Demo Mode**.
- Adopt a clean, text-centric design inspired by the **Apache Hadoop** style.
